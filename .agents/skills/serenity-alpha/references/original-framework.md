# Original Serenity Alpha Framework

This reference preserves the source framework from `a.md` in compact form.

## Essence

Do not ask whether a news item is big. Ask:

- Is there an already-observed demand change behind it?
- Can that demand enter a company's financial statements?
- Which company is small enough for the demand to change its fate?
- Is the market misclassifying the company?
- Can the thesis be verified through earnings within 1-4 quarters?

## Filters

Avoid news with:

- emotion but no demand
- real demand but only mega-cap beneficiaries
- fully priced-in winners
- overly long transmission chains
- no financial verification point

## Alpha Formula

```text
potential alpha strength =
  demand certainty
  x financial transmission clarity
  x business purity
  x market-cap elasticity
  x market neglect
```

Use small initial sizing for verifiable non-consensus ideas, then update with evidence.
