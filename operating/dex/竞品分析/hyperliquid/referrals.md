# Referrals

### How do I refer someone to Hyperliquid?

Create a referral code at [app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals](https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals) once you've hit $10,000 in volume. You'll earn 10% of your referred users' fees, minus any discount they receive.

Key limits:
- Referral rewards apply for a referred user's first $1B in volume
- Referral discounts apply for a referred user's first $25M in volume

Share via: `app.hyperliquid.xyz/join/YOURCODE`

Rewards accrue across all quote assets and can be claimed once they exceed $1. Claimed rewards show up in your spot balance.

---

### How do I use a referral code?

Enter a code at [app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals](https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals) or follow a friend's referral link. You'll get a "4% discount on your fees for your first $25M in volume."

Note: discounts don't apply to vaults or sub-accounts, as those are treated as independent accounts in the clearinghouse.
