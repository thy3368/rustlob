# Core contributors

Hyperliquid Labs leads the platform's development, with [Jeff](https://twitter.com/chameleon_jeff) and iliensinc—Harvard classmates—at the helm. The team includes members from Caltech and MIT with backgrounds at Airtable, Citadel, Hudson River Trading, and Nuro.

The group transitioned from proprietary crypto market making in 2020 to DeFi in summer 2022. They identified significant gaps in existing platforms: "poor market design, bad tech, and clunky UX." While trading these protocols proved profitable, the team recognized how far behind decentralized finance lagged compared to centralized alternatives.

Their mission centers on building a product addressing these shortcomings through a performant decentralized L1 featuring an integrated order book DEX. The team combines expertise in quantitative trading, blockchain technology, and user experience design to deliver this vision.

Community engagement is central to their approach—join the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/hyperliquid) to participate in discussions and provide feedback.

Notably, Hyperliquid Labs operates as a self-funded venture without external capital, enabling the team to prioritize product development aligned with their vision rather than external stakeholder demands.
