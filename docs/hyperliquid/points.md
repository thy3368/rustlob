# Points

The points program launched November 1, 2023, distributing 1,000,000 points weekly to users for six months through May 1, 2024.

Key details:
- Affiliates earned 1 point per 4 points their referred users earned
- Criteria updated on a recurring basis
- Weekly distributions based on activity ending Wednesdays at 00:00 UTC, paid out Thursdays
- Points also covered the closed alpha period through October 31, 2023

The L1 phase ran from May 29, 2024 through November 2024, with 700,000 points distributed weekly. Activity between May 1–28, 2024 received a multiplier. "Hyperliquid reserves the right to modify previous point distributions under its sole discretion."
